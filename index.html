How to use:
  • Give each pokemon a rating from 1 to 10 using the buttons below.
  • The tool automatically advances after each one, if you misclicked click the "go back" button.
  • When you get to the end, results will be shown. There are 1233 forms to rate, so make sure you have enough time to finish!
Notes:
  • Palette swap alt-forms are not listed seperately (arceus, deerling, florges, etc.).
  • Aegislash and Mimikyu's alt-forms are not listed separately, as they do not change shape and are merely posed differently.
  • Cramorant's alt-forms are not listed seperately, as it's the same bird just holding other pokemon.
  • Vivillon's alt-forms are not listed separately, as I cannot be bothered to do that many.
  • Palafin's zero form is not listed, as it's essentially identical to Finizen.
  • Maushold and Dudunsparce's extra alt-forms are not listed, since it's just the same thing but more.
  • Mega Latias and Mega Latios are the same, and therefore not listed separately.
  • Gigantamax Flapple and Gigantamax Appletun are the same, and therefore not listed separately.
  • All mega evolutions are listed as gen 6 pokemon.
  • All gigantamaxes are listed as gen 8 pokemon.
  • Primal reversion and eternamax count as mega evolution and gigantamax respectively, but ultra burst does not.
  • Stellar Terapagos is listed as normal type.
  • Ditto is listed as no eggs discovered egg group.
  • Gigantamax forms do not have shape or color listed separately in the pokedex, so they inherit them from their base form.
